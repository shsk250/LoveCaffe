Our Story

Founded in 2025, Love Cafe began as a passion project between three related coffee enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for specialty coffee with the world. We travel directly to coffee farms, building relationships with growers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Every bean we sell is carefully selected, freshly roasted, and delivered directly to your door. Our small-batch roasting process ensures peak flavor and aroma in every cup.
